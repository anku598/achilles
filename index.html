The Achilles Donkey Calf Raise attachment is the world’s 1st Calf Raise attachment for the Smith Machine. This Patented commercial fitness apparatus is Ergonomically designed to allow the user to exercise the (Gastrocnemius and Soleus) calf muscles. The Donkey Calf Raise is the most effective exercise for strengthening and building the calf muscle. It is a great isolation movement for overall calf development and calf mass and is simply one of the best exercises for developing the calves.
Achilles
The Donkey Calf Raise, if you're not familiar with it, is simply a calf raise done in a bent-forward position. Your upper body is leaning over at 90 degrees and is horizontal while your lower body remains vertical. The weight is placed on your lower back, then you just perform a calf raise from there! The reason this exercise is so productive is the strong stretch you can put on the calves at the bottom of every single rep because of how your body and legs are positioned during the exercise. You can hit the calves with heavy weight and leave your back out of the equation. Donkey calf raises are a forgotten exercise that I rarely see people doing. If you are looking to create a more Aesthetic physique or just perform better in sports, The Donkey Calf Raise exercise is a must!

Product Description
The ACHILLES Donkey Calf Raise Attachment is a high-intensity calf muscle trainer that works the calf muscle through a full range of motion. It tones, strengthens and builds the Calf muscles. The Donkey Calf Raise should be a mainstay of any effective calf-building routine.
You see, because the calf muscles (the gastrocnemius, to be specific) cross the knee joint, putting a stretch on the hamstrings also puts a greater stretch on the calves. So, bending over at the waist in this fashion will put a greater stretch on your calves than if you were standing completely straight up and down. But here's the problem...when you don't have a donkey calf raise machine or a partner to sit on your back (like they're riding a donkey, hence the name), how do you perform this exercise? Donkey Calf machines are not common in all gyms and if you're training at home, I have a strong feeling a donkey calf machine wasn't on your priority list of purchases. One of the solutions I've come up with is the Achilles Donkey Calf Raise attachment for the Smith machine.
